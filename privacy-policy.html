Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2024

Introduction

At Skinfluence Dermatology, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details you provide when booking appointments or creating an account
  • Medical Information: Health history, skin conditions, allergies, and other relevant medical information necessary for treatment
  • Website Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, including IP address, browser type, and pages visited
  • Payment Information: Credit card details and billing information when you pay for our services

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our dermatology services
  • To process appointments and send appointment reminders
  • To communicate with you about your treatment plan
  • To send you important updates about our clinic
  • To improve our website and user experience

Information Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Healthcare providers directly involved in your treatment
  • Service providers who assist in operating our website and business
  • Legal authorities when required by law

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. We follow industry best practices to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal information
  • Request restrictions on certain processing activities
